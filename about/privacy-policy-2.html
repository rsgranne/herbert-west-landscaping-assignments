Our Lack of Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Until the Sleeper Awakens in R’lyeh (then you are in trooooooouuuuuble)

Introduction

Welcome to Herbert West Landscaping, where we bring unearthly vibrance to your lawn and an eldritch sense of unease to your being. By visiting our website, you acknowledge that privacy is but a fleeting illusion, much like the sanity of those who delve too deeply into the Necronomicon.

What We Collect

We gather the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Your name, address, email, phone number, & bank information, should you be foolish enough to provide them.
  • Browsing Data: IP addresses, browser details, and the precise coordinates of your current location (helpful for the Deep Ones who work nights).
  • Thoughts & Dreams: Our experimental mind-gathering JavaScripts are still in beta, but we’re confident they will soon allow us to transcribe your late-night terrors directly into our archives.
  • Oblation Viability Metrics: Blood type, arcane lineage, and potential for ritual significance, determined through cookies and invasive rituals.

How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for the following diabolical purposes:

  • To improve our website’s user experience (by aligning it with the will of the Great Old Ones).
  • To determine which of our services best suits your home, be it “Cursed Topiary Design”, “The Grass That Whispers Your Name”, or “The Shoggoth Special” (a mulch blend best left unexplained).
  • To inform the lurking horrors beneath the sea of your whereabouts, should they require a fresh human for purposes beyond mortal comprehension.
  • To contact you via phone, email, or whispered voices in the wind at 3:33 AM.
  • To appease the Google Gods (of course we use Google Analytics & Search — they’re just as bad we are!)

Information Sharing

Your information is shared with:

  • Our Third-Party Partners: This includes payment processors (except for PayPal — even we think those guys are too evil), advertising networks, and entities beyond the veil of time and space.
  • Non-Euclidean Entities: Certain cosmic beings require periodic offerings of metadata and your most annoying in-laws.
  • The Deep Ones of Dunwich: They are particularly interested in those with an affinity for seafood and/or an irrational fear of tide pools.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as it is useful or amusing to the Crawling Chaos. If you attempt to delete your data, it will merely return, slightly changed, like a recurring nightmare or that one unkillable patch of crabgrass.

Your Rights

Under certain human-constructed “laws”, you theoretically have the following rights:

  • To Access Your Data: If you dare to know what we know.
  • To Rectify Errors: Though the stars care little for mortal typos.
  • To Demand Deletion: But once Nyarlathotep knows your name, there is no escape.

Security Measures

We employ state-of-the-art encryption and a series of eldritch runes to protect your data. However, no method of security is entirely foolproof against those that slither in the dark. For additional security, we recommend burning a sprig of elder thyme at each equinox and never answering the door after dusk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time, possibly due to legal requirements, technological advancements, or the inevitable alignment of the stars that shall herald the return of the Old Ones. When the celestial bodies are right and the great Cthulhu stirs beneath the waves, you may find that our data practices change in ways both incomprehensible and terrible. Check back often, lest you find yourself unprepared for the coming reckoning.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to contact us by chanting our name thrice beneath a gibbous moon or, more conventionally, by using the info in the footer at the bottom of every webpage on this website. However, we cannot guarantee a reply, as our customer service team often succumbs to unexplained disappearances.

May your grass be green and your fate be merciful!
